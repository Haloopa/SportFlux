Our Product

There's is our software and hardware

Home

This is the homepage of the application we are developing

  • There is a user identity.
  • Live recording from the device.
  • There is a list of devices connected to the application.
  • And there are menu options that can be accessed by users.

Physioterapis

This is a menu view of physiotherapists, which can help us to consult with them online. We will also help to recommend the best physiotherapist.

Trainer

In this trainer menu, we can be helped to find the best trainer and can consult about sports and even strategies that must be used for competitions and in daily life in order to avoid sports injuries.

Level Bar

The front view of the hardware we are developing is as follows.

  • There is a level bar, an indicator light that is colored blue to red. To display the level of muscle energy recorded by the EMG sensor.
  • Logo. To display the brand identity of the product.

Indicator

Display on the top of the hardware

  • Here there is a battery indicator to display the battery power level.
  • Then there is the connection indicator to display the connection level between the SportFlux EMG and the SportFlux Mobile App.
  • And there is a power button to activate the hardware.

Port Type C

Displayed on the side of the hardware, there is a type c port for charging the device's battery.

Gel

To capture electrical signals from muscle activity during contraction and relaxation.
